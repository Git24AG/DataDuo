Our Story

Following our successful charity event on September 26, 2021, at the Platres sports facilities, the Platres3on3 Group joyfully announces our donation of â‚¬3,000 (Three Thousand Euros) to Europa Donna Cyprus.

Through this initiative, we expressed unwavering support and love to every member of Europa Donna Cyprus. Although our endeavor lasted a mere few weeks, it provided an opportunity for individuals of all ages across Cyprus to contribute to our collective effort in their unique ways.

Beyond the game of basketball, our aim was to intensify the fight for life alongside those battling cancer. Their struggle is our struggle.

We are immensely proud of our accomplishments and extend heartfelt gratitude to our invaluable sponsors, dedicated volunteers, and passionate participants. Their altruism, selfless assistance, and social responsibility were truly touching.

Despite the challenges posed by Covid-19 restrictions, our event on September 26, 2021 was a resounding success. It was not only celebrated by the public but also endorsed by the media team accompanying us, suggesting its transformation into an annual institution.

May our past contributions have brought hope and smiles, bolstering morale and acting as a shield of protection for our fellow human beings, as we look forward to continuing this legacy in the upcoming charity event.

In conclusion, we extend warm thanks to our sponsors for their impeccable cooperation in the past and call upon them once again to contribute to the new event scheduled for next month. We eagerly anticipate future collaborations and the positive impact they will bring to our cause.
